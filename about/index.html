About me

I studied statistics at Oregon State University (Corvallis, Oregon) were it drizzles with cold water on average 162 days per year and now I live in Ho Chi Minh City (Vietnam). My first professional job was being a receptionist at OSU, which lasted for 5 years. After that, I worked as a teaching and research assistant in the Statistics Department, and then an analyst for the Student Affairs research unit, also at OSU. I am interested in applying the skills I learn in statistics and data analytics to improving human affairs such as education and human resources.
